What is an
immersive show?

Traditional shows have a tight framework — the time of the show is limited, the plot is predetermined, actors play on stage, the audience watches in the hall.

The immersive show lasts all day and takes place «in the crowd» — the audience becomes participants and their reaction changes the plot — actors constantly improvise immersing the audience even deeper and deeper.

The good show takes a person out of reality into a fantasy world by offering a vivid plot, thoughtful scenery and psychological tricks, swinging emotions from constrained modesty and cautious curiosity to euphoria and the triumph of their own dashing!

UFO IN THE HOTEL

THE HARD ONE

DESCRIPTION

Late at night, there’s something strange happening nearby the hotel — strange sounds, a flash, people running around with flashlights...

In the morning guests will see a cube of transparent plastic wrap on their way to the restaurant. Inside the cube there is a couple of people wearing special suits and they are making some strange manipulations with «SOMETHING» on the table — green «blood» sprinkles onto the wrapping from the inside...

After coming to the restaurant they will find the «instruction» on how to behave with the aliens, the gummy-bear candies as an «anti-aliens pills» and the «foil hats» to protect their brains.

It becomes clear that there was a UFO landing near the hotel and a special team is busy finding and investigating them...

  • Further during the day, almost continuously, thematic activities and rallies take place, for example:
  • a pop-up shop with «alien souvenirs»
  • bartenders furtively offer «special cocktails from alien irradiation»
  • «military officers» recruit children volunteers into «space troops» where they are trained to shoot water guns and various physical training for «a possible fight against aliens».
  • alien DNA infection testing and «disinfection» at the nearest bar of course
  • a tent with predictions from a representative of the «highest alien race»
  • evening show/disco for all guests with an «alien» face painting and masquerade
  • showing the video compilation with reactions of guests to what is happening during the day - a collage shot with a hidden camera and compiled with guests' own social networks videos

SHOOTING STARS

THE EASY ONE

DESCRIPTION

In the lobby of the hotel, guests will be greeted by a cardboard 3D-stand familiar from movie theaters, with announcement of making-off a video clip for the Star that is being prepared at the hotel, and those who wish can take part in the crowd and several supporting roles after a short casting.

Filming process is interesting for everyone, even just to watch from aside, and most daring ones can also participate!

In the morning filming equipment, makeup trailers, costumes and props is being to arrive to the hotel: everything suggests that preparation for a serious filming process begins.

The participants are gathered, divided into groups, the script is explained and they begin to rehearse. The film crew builds the scenery around as quickly as possible, delivers props, prepares costumes.

A director appears on the set and from that moment on, everything starts to look like armageddon. The director behaves extremely courteously with guests and is just awful with his team. Because of him, various funny and «unfortunate» cases occur during filming: props are falling, costumes are torn, the director’s wife is taken by surprise kissing right behind the curtains.

Guests witness dramas that are constantly arising here and there: personal and professional, career ups and downs, even an «ambulance» will come several times!

The final scene is the filming of an evening disco with a group dance that is being prepared during the day. We will reveal the secret and show a funny video with the events of the day — a collage shot with a hidden camera and a video of the guests themselves from the social networks.
