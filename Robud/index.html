User app

RODUD is a free simple app. It connects you directly with trucks (flatbed – sides – curtains) owners and drivers per hour and in all the kingdom cities. You could transport abroad if you wish. Moreover the shipment is insured and you can track the truck easily.

The most important is that the price is like the market and better. When you have more orders on the app, you’ll get gifts like amazing tablets and mobile phones.

How it works?

RODUD safe transportation – quick response

You can download RODUD app on google play and apple store.

Then sign up with your mobile phone. And you get a verification code.

Now you’re ready to work. Set the start point and the kind of the truck you want and drop-off point then the price comes and if you press accept the driver comes to you.

Driver app

RODUD is a simple app that connects you with factories and companies that have goods to transport per hour and all the week, the prices are like the market and you take your money on your hand.

Moreover the shipment is insured and if the company has a big number of trucks, it takes a control panel to watch everything, where the driver upload, where he goes, how much he gets, and more. No more you come back empty after you download your shipment like now.

All this for 5% from the price of the trip you take on the app.

How it works?

You can download RODUD app on google play and apple store.

Then you sign up with your mobile phone and you get a verification code.

Add your truck information ( flatbed – sides – curtain).

Upload your ID photo, driving license, the truck license, transportation card, the truck photo.

Now you are ready, open the app when you search for a shipment and wait to get orders.
